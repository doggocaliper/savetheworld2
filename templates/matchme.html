Confused about what is your sexual orientation?
Here is a list of sexual orientations available in this world:



Sexual orientation Description
Heterosexual Attraction to individuals of the opposite sex. This is the most common sexual orientation.
Homosexual Attraction to individuals of the same sex. This includes gay men, lesbian women, and other individuals who identify as part of the LGBTQ+ community.
Bisexual Attraction to individuals of both sexes. This can also include individuals who are attracted to people regardless of their gender identity.
Pansexual Attraction to individuals of all genders and gender identities.
Asexual Lack of sexual attraction to any gender. Some asexual individuals may still experience romantic attraction.
Demisexual Attraction only after a strong emotional connection has been established.
Queer A term that can be used to describe anyone who identifies as part of the LGBTQ+ community, but does not fit neatly into any of the other categories.
